About This Campaign

We're raising funds to support students with urgent medical emergencies including surgeries, treatments, and hospital expenses. Our goal is to ensure that no student has to compromise their education due to medical financial constraints.

The funds will be allocated to students based on the severity of their medical conditions, financial background, and urgency of treatment. All applications will be reviewed by a committee of medical professionals and university administrators.
